Background Story

Our team decides to make a lego robot car out of CV6. Through brainstorming, our group finds doing a delivery system model would be a novel idea. We will use logo robot car as our delivery car. In addition, we will make our own maps to simulate a real map. This website is used to show all details about the lego car, as well as allowing user to input the delivery they want. We will in total have 6 houses with different color. The lego robot will follow a specific path and deliver the package to the corrosponding color.
